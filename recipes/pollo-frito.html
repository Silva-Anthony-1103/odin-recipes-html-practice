Fried Chicken

Description

Classic Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Avocado Oil
  • Eggs
  • Breading
  • Flour
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Coat chicken in seasoned flour, then egg wash, then breading. Repeat this step once more
  2. Fry chicken in 350 degree avocado oil until golden brown
  3. Rest for 5 minutes. VERY IMPORTANT!!!
  4. Enjoy!
