Delicious Italian Hoagie

Description

A yummy sandwich classic of deli meats transporting you straight to the Mediterranean beaches of Italy.

Ingredients

  • Sandwich Roll
  • Salami, Turkey, Ham
  • Lettuce, Olives, Banana peppers
  • Provolone
  • Mayo, Italian Dressing

Steps

  1. Put mayo on roll
  2. Layer meat on mayo
  3. Layer provolone on meat
  4. Cover provolone with veggies
  5. Douse in Italian Dresing
  6. Close sandwich and bake at 400 degrees F for 5 minutes
  7. Let it cool down and enjoy!
