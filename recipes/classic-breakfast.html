Classic American Breakfast

Description

Classic American Breakfast of Waffles, Eggs (however you like them), Bacon, Sausage, and Hashbrowns.

Ingredients

  • Waffle Mix (Premade or do it yourself)
  • Maple Syrup
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Sausage
  • Hashbrowns
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Cook waffle mix in waffle iron
  2. Cook seasoned eggs however you like them
  3. Cook bacon and sausage
  4. Cook seasoned hashbrowns
  5. Assemble and Enjoy!
